Walter Elias Disney

"The way to get started is to quit talking and begin doing"

Here's a time line of Walt Disney's life:

  • 1901 - Born in Chicago, Illinois
  • 1917 - At 16, joined the Red Cross as an ambulance driver
  • 1919 - Draftsman at Kansas City Art Studio
  • 1920 - Disney and Iwerks formed a short-lived company called Iwerks-Disney Commercial Artists
  • 1923 - Opened Walt Disney Studios alongside his brother Roy
  • 1928 - Steamboat Willie premiere staring Mickey Mouse
  • 1934 - Began to work on Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs
  • 1940's - New studio at Fairbank, where Pinnochio, Fantasia, Dumbo and Bamby were produced
  • 1955 - Inauguration of the children's amusement park DisneyLand
  • 1965 - Musical Mary Poppins scored 30 nominations and 5 academy awards
  • 1966 - Dies victim of lung cancer
